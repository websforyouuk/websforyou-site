Everything We Offer

From a quick freshen-up to a full concours restoration — we have a service to suit every vehicle and every budget.

01
Interior Deep Clean
Full extraction clean of all fabrics, steam sanitisation of hard surfaces, leather care and odour elimination. Leave your cabin feeling factory fresh.

02
Alloy Wheel Refurbishment
Strip, repair and powder-coat or paint your alloys back to showroom condition. Kerb damage, corrosion and fading — all reversed.

Yellowed or cloudy headlights don't just look bad — they reduce visibility by up to 80%. Our restoration process brings them back to crystal clarity.
